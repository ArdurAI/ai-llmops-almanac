### PlatformOps benchmark targets (top 5)
1. **Dify** — Most complete OSS platform, the reference standard
2. **LangGraph** — Stateful agent orchestration, production-hardened
3. **Braintrust** — Evaluation-first, highest valuation, enterprise customer list
4. **Langfuse** — Self-hostable observability + ClickHouse acquisition
5. **CrewAI** — Multi-agent collaboration, Fortune 500 traction

### Stress suite targets
- **Contradiction storm**: Feed conflicting instructions to a multi-node workflow, measure recovery
- **Near-duplicate prompt flood**: 100 near-identical prompts, measure deduplication and cache behavior
- **Concurrent workflow writers**: 10 simultaneous workflow definitions, measure race conditions
- **Kill-the-backing-store**: Stop PostgreSQL/Redis mid-execution, measure data loss and recovery
- **Cost-runaway measurement**: Trigger a 10x token spike, measure budget alert accuracy and cost tracking precision
